Home Privacy Terms

Contact

Email support at help@legifax.com.

We aim to respond as soon as practical.

What To Include

  • A short description of the issue.
  • Your account email if you are signed in.
  • The recipient fax number involved, if the issue is about a specific send.
  • The approximate send time and status shown in the app.
  • Any job, status, receipt, or error details shown on the app screen.
  • Your device model and iOS version if the issue is device-specific.
  • Screenshots are helpful, but please avoid sending sensitive document content unless needed for support.

Subscriptions

LegiFax Pro purchases and subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To cancel or manage a subscription, open iOS Settings, tap your Apple ID, then Subscriptions.

If a purchase does not unlock, try Restore Purchases in the app before contacting support.

Fax Delivery

If a fax is delayed, waiting, or failed, check the status screen in the app first. Fax delivery may depend on recipient fax machines, fax network conditions, recipient organization systems, and third-party delivery providers.

For legally, medically, financially, or professionally important documents, confirm receipt with the recipient directly.

Privacy And Account Deletion

You can delete your LegiFax account in the app from Settings > Delete account.

For privacy questions or account deletion help, email help@legifax.com.
