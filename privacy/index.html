LegiFax

Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information LegiFax stores or processes when you use the app to prepare and send faxes.

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Home Terms Support

Summary

LegiFax is designed for sending faxes from a mobile device. LegiFax does not sell your personal information.

Because fax delivery requires cloud processing, some document, account, recipient, purchase, and delivery information is processed by LegiFax backend systems and service providers.

Information You Add To The App

You may enter, import, scan, upload, or create information such as:

  • Document images, imported files, page previews, annotations, signatures, and final fax PDFs.
  • Recipient fax numbers and related send details.
  • Account identifiers, email address, sign-in state, support details, and purchase status.
  • Delivery status, timestamps, provider job identifiers, receipt metadata, failure reasons, and app diagnostic details needed for reliability and support.

This information is used to provide the app's core functionality: preparing documents, submitting fax jobs, tracking delivery, showing sent-fax history, generating receipts, protecting paid access, preventing abuse, reconciling provider events, and supporting users.

Fax Delivery

Final fax PDFs and related page files are uploaded to LegiFax backend systems so they can be transmitted for fax delivery.

LegiFax uses Telnyx as its production fax delivery provider. Final fax PDFs, recipient fax numbers, delivery requests, sender information needed for delivery, provider job identifiers, delivery status, and provider error details may be sent to or processed by Telnyx solely to deliver, track, reconcile, troubleshoot, and support fax delivery.

The recipient at the destination fax number can see the fax content. LegiFax cannot control how the recipient or recipient organization handles a fax after delivery.

Backend And Storage

LegiFax uses Firebase and Google Cloud services for backend API requests, account data, storage, fax job records, delivery status, receipt metadata, and operational logs.

Recipient fax numbers, sender or account identifiers, delivery state, timestamps, provider job identifiers, and support metadata may be stored for delivery, history, receipts, fraud prevention, provider reconciliation, and support.

Authentication

LegiFax may use Clerk for sign-in and authentication. If you sign in, Clerk may process identifiers such as your email address, Apple account sign-in information, authentication status, and related technical data needed to provide login services.

If you use Sign in with Apple, Apple may provide LegiFax with an Apple private relay email address ending in privaterelay.appleid.com instead of your personal email address.

Subscriptions And Purchases

LegiFax may offer LegiFax Pro through Apple in-app purchase subscriptions. Apple handles payment processing, billing, refunds, and subscription management. RevenueCat may be used to manage subscription status, purchase state, and app entitlements.

We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

Optional Analytics

Anonymous usage analytics are off by default. If you choose to share anonymous usage data in Settings, LegiFax may use PostHog to process app interaction events, screen flow, feature usage, app version, device type, operating system, locale, anonymous installation identifiers, and diagnostic metadata.

Analytics events are designed not to include fax document content, imported file names, recipient fax numbers, sender phone numbers, raw error messages, payment card details, or authentication secrets. You can turn analytics sharing on or off in Settings at any time.

Device And Technical Information

LegiFax and its service providers may process device, app, and technical information such as app version, operating system, device type, locale, anonymous installation identifiers, request identifiers, error diagnostics, purchase state, authentication state, and delivery status.

Data Retention

Final fax PDFs and related stored fax files are retained for up to 30 days, unless they are deleted earlier through account deletion or operational cleanup.

Sent-fax metadata and receipt state may remain available for account history until account deletion. Minimal non-content audit records may be retained for fraud prevention and fax provider reconciliation.

Deletion and audit records expire after 90 days where configured. Information processed by Apple, Clerk, RevenueCat, Firebase, Google Cloud, Telnyx, or PostHog may also be handled according to those services' retention policies.

Account Deletion

You can delete your LegiFax account in the app from Settings. Account deletion deletes LegiFax account data and sent fax files from LegiFax-controlled systems, subject to legally or operationally necessary non-content audit records.

Deleting your LegiFax account does not cancel App Store billing. Manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID settings.

Your Choices

  • You can choose what documents and recipient fax numbers you provide.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID settings.
  • You can turn optional analytics sharing on or off in Settings.
  • You can use in-app account deletion to remove LegiFax account data and sent fax files.
  • You can contact us for privacy or support questions.

Children's Privacy

LegiFax is intended for business, professional, and personal document use and is not directed to children.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as LegiFax changes. The updated version will show a new last updated date.

Contact
